How it works?

With findMEreadME you can both share your books and read the books of others. To share your book, you just need to create a book profile. To read books of other members, you need to search for them and find them.

Have fun in only a few steps!

Create Profile

Create your personal profile to have a full access to findMEreadME. List through all registered book and explore them. Sign up

Book Registration

Pick up a book from your bookshelf you wish to share with others. Follow the registration guide to create a book profile. Finally, hide the book or give it to a friend and wait for the reactions!

Book Journey

Follow the journey of your book around the world. Read the comments or check the current book location - maybe it will come back to you!
